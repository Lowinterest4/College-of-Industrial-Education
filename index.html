About CIT

The College of Industrial Technology (CIT) is committed to developing students into highly skilled, industry-ready professionals. It offers strong technical programs designed to enhance creativity, problem-solving abilities, and hands-on expertise essential in today's modern workforce.

Purpose of CIT

The purpose of CIT is to equip learners with the knowledge, craftsmanship, and professional work ethics needed for success in technical industries. It trains students to become competent technicians, innovators, and leaders who can contribute effectively to industrial and technological advancement.

Benefits and Opportunities

  • Practical, hands-on laboratory and workshop experience
  • Highly trained and industry-experienced instructors
  • Updated tools, equipment, and facilities
  • Strong technical and problem-solving skill development
  • Opportunities for internships, industry linkage, and job placements
  • Exposure to real-world projects and applications

CIT GOAL

The College of Industrial Technology aims to produce technologically proficient, value-driven, and globally competitive graduates. It commits to shaping individuals equipped with advanced technical skills, professional discipline, and positive work ethics necessary to excel in various industrial and technological fields.
